About Whoosh Wushu Center

Established in May 2023, Whoosh Wushu Center is a dynamic martial arts training and performance team based in Cheras Selatan 118. With over a decade of experience, our performers blend traditional and modern Wushu techniques. We have delivered impressive performances in more than 10 large-scale events and many smaller showcases.

Our Core Values

  • Fusion of strength, artistry, and combat precision
  • Celebrating the heritage and evolution of Wushu
  • Delivering high-impact, visually stunning performances

Lion Dance

Traditional and acrobatic lion dance performances suitable for celebrations and corporate events. Symbolizing prosperity, strength, and good fortune. Ideal for festivals, weddings, and grand openings.

Weaponry & Combat

Dynamic fight sequences using swords, spears, staffs, and bows. Action-packed, precise, and narratively engaging.

Archery Displays

Choreographed archery performances with dramatic formations and synchronized movements. Great for cultural and fantasy-themed events.

Theatrical Martial Arts

We combine martial arts with storytelling, costumes, and music for an immersive stage experience. Perfect for themed shows and special showcases.

Modern Acrobatics

Includes flips, aerials, and cinematic stunt work. Energetic routines suited for commercials, concerts, and media appearances.

Taichi Performances

Graceful, meditative, and powerful movements reflecting balance and harmony. Ideal for wellness events and formal ceremonies.
